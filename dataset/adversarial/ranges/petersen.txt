# Petersen graph: 10 nodes, χ=3, 3-regular, girth 5, triangle-free.
# Hardest known 3-chromatic 3-regular graph. Classic adversary for greedy.
# All 10 nodes form one BCC — forces backtracking to find 3-coloring.
# With N=3: feasible. With N=2: infeasible.
# Outer cycle a-b-c-d-e-a. Inner star f-h-j-g-i-f. Spokes a-f,b-g,c-h,d-i,e-j.
